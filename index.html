This Privacy Policy describes how MenuGreen ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our mobile application MenuGreen (the "Service").

By downloading, installing, or using the Service, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information You Provide

When you create an account or use our Service, we may collect:

  • Email address
  • Full name
  • Password (stored securely using encryption/hashing)
  • Profile picture (optional)
  • Phone number (optional)

1.2 Health & Nutrition Data

Because MenuGreen provides calorie calculation and food recommendation features, we may collect:

  • Daily calorie intake
  • Food consumption history
  • Dietary preferences and restrictions
  • Health goals (e.g., weight loss, weight gain, maintenance)

Note: This data is used solely to provide personalized nutrition recommendations and is not shared with third parties for advertising.

1.3 Usage Data

We automatically collect certain information when you use the Service:

  • Device information (model, OS version, device ID)
  • IP address
  • App version
  • Pages/screens viewed
  • Features used
  • Time and date of your visits

1.4 Order & Transaction Data

When you place an order, we collect:

  • Order details (items, quantities, prices)
  • Delivery address
  • Order history
  • Transaction ID (payment processing only - we do NOT store full payment card or bank account information)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

a) To provide and maintain the Service

  • Create and manage your account
  • Process food orders and deliveries
  • Calculate calories and provide food recommendations
  • Track your dietary history

b) To improve user experience

  • Personalize food recommendations based on your preferences
  • Remember your favorite restaurants and dishes
  • Display relevant content

c) To communicate with you

  • Send order confirmations and delivery updates
  • Send push notifications about order status, promotions (with your consent)
  • Respond to your inquiries and support requests

d) To process payments

  • Facilitate secure payment transactions through SePay

e) For security and analytics

  • Detect and prevent fraud
  • Analyze usage patterns to improve the Service
  • Diagnose technical issues

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Data Storage

Your data is stored on:

  • Amazon Web Services (AWS) - Relational Database Service (RDS)
  • Database engine: PostgreSQL
  • Region: Singapore (ap-southeast-1)
  • Firebase Authentication (for account management)

3.2 Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • SSL/TLS encryption for data in transit
  • AES-256 encryption for data at rest
  • Password hashing using bcrypt
  • Regular database backups
  • Access controls and authentication for all database access
  • Secure API endpoints with token-based authentication

However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.
